03/20/2020

*MAJOR UPDATE*

Hello everyone! The admins have elected to change our name to "Folding at Home Team 240440: Let's Help Stop COVID-19" to reflect the fact that we are not a group run by the official Folding at Home labs, and make our team number more accessible. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns! I never expected this to blow up like it did, I thought it would just be a few friends and I, but here we are! And we are not alone. Before the coronavirus, about 30,000 people were working on F@H, but now we are at 400,000 people worldwide. According to Dr. Greg Bowman, the director of F@H, our total computing power now exceeds 470 petaflops - 2x faster than the worlds fastest supercomputer. This is incredible! Summit has already identified 77 new compounds that may be viable as therapies for the coronavirus. We may be a bit behind on drug screening time-wise, but we have double the total computing power. Let's see what we can do! It looks like F@H is getting the attraction of the media. See more on that here. Also, the F@H has a goal of getting 1 million people folding! Let's try to spread the word on social media and by word of mouth. As a final (and highly important note) the spike protein and how it moves has been resolved, courtesy of us and everyone else all around the world! To my knowledge, an atom-by-atom simulation of this protein has never been done before. This protein is what the virus uses to get into your cells, and is currently the most attractive target for developing therapies. To watch it in motion, please check out Dr. Bowman's twitter post here. There is more to come! This is just the beginning. We are making tangible, critical progress. I believe the next step is to get a more detailed image of the viral spike protein, start to simulate other viral proteins, and screen drugs against the new protein models that we have come up with to see if they can inhibit them and possibly work as therapies down the line. More updates to come, but in the meantime, please share with friends! Thank you for all your time and effort that you are putting into this! It may just entail downloading a program and watching it run right now, but this has the potential to become something really important and meaningful for all of us. Let's keep folding!

-Joe
